Portfolio

3D Rendering

Over the last several years I've learned a bit of 3D modeling and rendering using DAZ Studio. This has especially taken off with the advent of Nvidia Iray. My modeling skills are still fairly rudimentary, but I find that a lot of skills in producing 3D scenes translates directly from photography. I mostly do Sci-Fi and Fantasy renders.

Meadowlark Gardens

Meadowlark Botanical Gardens in Vienna, Virginia. Lots of local flora and fauna can be seen among lakes, forests, and walking trails. It's is also a a favorite location for weddings and receptions, so it's not unusual to come upon one when you visit. In addition, Meadowlark Gardens hosts photography and other outdoor events.

HDR

High dynamic range (HDR) photography is created with multiple exposures with different shutter settings to pull more detail from the shadows and highlights than would be possible with a single protograph.
